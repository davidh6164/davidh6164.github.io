Hello, my name is David Horn and I am a Web Designer. I focus on UI Design, but I also develop small to medium size websites for individuals, companies and organizations.

Blue Fox Furnishings
Website | Web Design

Blue Fox Furnishings is a Rustic Wood Furnishings company. They were originally selling their products through the Etsy Marketplace, but as their business expanded they wanted to grow as well. I helped them launch their first website where you can buy products directly from Blue Fox Furnishings. This marketplace takes advantage of what the Magento CMS has to offer in terms of security and functionality. I built the custom theme using Bootstrap and jQuery.

The Locks
Web Theme

A website theme designed for a musician or band. Built with Bootstrap and jQuery. This website theme's purpose is to serve as a musician's hub. Whether its showcasing the music, the artists, or exposing their music and spotlight with the assistance of social media.

Toro Bailando
Web Theme

Toro Bailando is a website theme designed for restaurants. Features like an interactive menu and an image gallery to display foods and the ambiance of the eatery. I coded the website theme using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap and jQuery. I also included an online contact form and a reservation form to increase interactivity between the website and the web visitor to generate business.

About Me

I am a passionate freelance web designer who wonders about websites and how their designs affect their relationship with users. I will help you solve your web design problems whether it is prototyping designs or user testing. You can get into contact with me on my Contact web page or on Twitter.
